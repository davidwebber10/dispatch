# Dispatch v2.8.7 — Chat & control polish

## The change

Follow-up polish to Pretty mode and the sidebar controls.

- **User images sit on the user's side.** A pasted or attached screenshot rendered
  on the left (assistant) side of the chat, like a tool's output. It now renders
  right-aligned with the human's turn, its caption in the bubble just above it.
- **Sort and `+` are one icon family.** The sort control and the add-`+` buttons
  were two different text glyphs (Arial ⇅ vs. Plex `+`), so no amount of
  font-matching made them look like siblings. Both are now Phosphor icons —
  `ArrowsDownUp` and `Plus`, same weight, size-matched — on mobile **and** desktop.
- **The CLI ⇄ Pretty switcher greys out when it's locked.** Mid-turn (or before a
  thread has a session), switching is disabled — but the control still looked fully
  active. The whole switcher now dims so its unavailability reads at a glance.
- **The ★ Insight callout loses its accent left border** — it keeps a clean,
  uniform 1px border instead.

## Under the hood

- `renderTimeline` gets a dedicated branch for `imageFromUser` images: they flush
  the assistant group and render right-aligned like a `UserBubble`, while
  tool-output images stay left in the assistant column.
- `SortMenu` now renders `<ArrowsDownUp>` and takes an `iconSize` so each host
  passes the same size as its own `<Plus>`; `SORT_GLYPH`/`SORT_GLYPH_FONT` are
  gone. Every text-`+` button (ProjectCard, ProjectSidebar, EmptyWorkspace) became
  `<Plus>`. The numeric stepper `+`/`−` in settings is left alone — it's a
  different control.
- `TransportToggle` dims the whole group whenever it can't switch (`busy || !canSwitch`).
- `InsightCallout` drops its `borderLeft`.

All of this is web-only — a browser refresh surfaces it; no daemon restart needed.
